\subsection{Routing}
\begin{itemize}[nosep]
    \item Routing is the process of updating forwarding tables
          \begin{itemize}[nosep]
              \item Routers exchange messages about routers or networks they can reach
              \item Goal: find optimal route for every destination
              \item \dots or maybe a good route, or \emph{any} route (depending on scale)
          \end{itemize}
    \item Challenges
          \begin{itemize}[nosep]
              \item Dynamic topology
              \item Decentralized
              \item Scale
          \end{itemize}
\end{itemize}
